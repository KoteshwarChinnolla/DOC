\section{Summary \& Research Statement}

Electronics and Communication Engineering graduate specializing in AI/ML, with a focus on real-world autonomous systems and cloud-native software platforms. My work has evolved from early experimentation in machine learning into the design and implementation of applied AI systems that operate in practical environments. Publishing a research paper on \textit{"Natural Language Based Autonomous Navigation Vehicle"} at IEEE ICOCT 2025 strengthened both my technical foundation and research approach. Alongside academic work, over one year of industry experience has deepened my knowledge of production-scale systems, Kubernetes, backend development, and AWS cloud services.

\smallskip
